What is the process of creativity?
The process of coming up with new, different ideas, or to put old ideas into a new perspective.

What are the specific steps involved?
a. Figure out what you want to get more creative at. (coding, painting, sculpting, etc.).

b. Next, figure out how to do the thing you want to get more creative at.

c. Try said thing the first time.

d. If you enjoy doing whatever it is, continue doing it.

e. As you continue doing it, try and make your topics and ideas more and more diverse.
